RULES OF THE GAME: Players take turns gifting villagers in order to make friends. There are four dice. If you roll 14 or more, the villager becomes your friend and you may roll again. If you roll 12-13, nothing happens. If you roll 6-11, you lose a friend. If you roll 5 or less, you lose all your friends. For all rolls that are 5-15, your turn ends and it becomes the other player’s turn. There is a pass option for if you’re on a friend streak but feel like your luck may be running out. The first to make it to 10 friends wins!
