Rules: At the start of the game, the starting player is randomly chosen. Players take turns gifting villagers in order to make friends. The first to make 7 friends wins!

Roll effects:

  • 14+: You gain a friend and you get to roll again!
  • 12-13: Nothing happens and it becomes the other player's turn.
  • 6-11: You lose a friend and it becomes the other player's turn.
  • 4-5: You lose all of your friends and it becomes the other player's turn.

To view the rules during the game, interact with the info button that will be located at the top right of the screen. ☺
